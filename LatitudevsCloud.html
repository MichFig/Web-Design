What does it mean if something is described as cloudy?

  1. :of, relating to, or resembling a cloud
  2. :darkened by gloom or anxiety
  3. :overcast with clouds
  4. :dimmed or dulled as if by clouds
  5. :uneven in color or texture
  6. :having visible material in suspension
