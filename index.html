A Study of Weather Patterns

Welcome to Michelle's Weather Analysis Page. The purpose of this page is to showcase our limited web design abilities, utilizing the images of a prior assignment.
Specifically, the weather data from multiple global cities randomly assigned and collected through the OpenWeather API. This weather data was then
plotted by latitude and compared to additional weather variables of max temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speeds for visualazation.

Below you will find links to the raw data, as well as links to additioanl information on the individual plots and studies. Each study will take you to a separate page for further explanation.
