Why Is My Hair So Frizzy Today?




Humidity is the amount of water vapor in the air. If the amount is high, humidiy will be high. Conversly, if the amount is low, humidity will be low.
It is what gives the are that wet, sticky feeling. On weather reports, humidity is explained as "Relative Humidity" which is actually a ratio between the
amount of water vapor in the air and the air's saturation point. Relative humidity is expressed as a percentage.


Humidity brings to mind, heat. Heat brings to mind tropical temperatures. One could hypothesize that relative humidity would be highest
around the equator. However, as is shown by the plot, high humidity is present globally. Latitude does not affect percentage.
